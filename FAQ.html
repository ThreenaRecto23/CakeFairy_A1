Does your product healthy and clean?

How Ingredients Being Processed

1

Get Ingredients


Ingredients are supplied by trusted supplier.

2

Ensures Quality


The quality and freshness of the ingredients are being examined first before delivering them to the food storage. Rejected ingredients are classified biodegradable waste.

3

Sanitization


To make sure that the ingredients are clean and virus-free, carefull sanitization are being made.

4

Storing


Ingredients before use are stored in a clean room with enough temperature. They are stored an hours before they are processed and serve.
